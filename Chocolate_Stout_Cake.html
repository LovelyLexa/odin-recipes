Chocolate Stout Cake

What's meal without dessert? Here I'll instruct you on how to make the best chocolate stout cake.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unbleached all purpose flour
  • 3/4 cup whole-wheat pastry flour
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground allspice
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tsps ground flax seed
  • 2/3 cup unsweetend almond milk
  • 1/2 cup dark beer
  • 1/3 cup oil
  • 3 TBSPs maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 3 TBSPs dark chocolate chips
  • 1 TBSP powdered sugar, for topping

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk flours, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, salt, allspice, cinnamon, and flaxseed.
  3. Slowly whisk in almond milk, beer, oil, maple syrup, and vanilla into dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Grease Bundt pan and pour batter filling 3/4 full.
  6. Bake for 30 to 32 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Cool and dust powdered sugar on top.
