Sesame Miso Soup with Ginger and Tofu

This is the easiest, fastest miso soup I've ever made. Delicious too!

Ingredients

  • 2 tsps oil
  • 6 ounces firl tofu
  • 6 shiitake mushrooms, stemmed and julienned.
  • 1 small carrot shredded
  • 5 cups vegetable broth
  • 1/3 cup light miso paste
  • 2 tsps toasted sesame oil
  • 2 tsps tamari
  • 2 tsps fresh ginger, grated
  • 4 scallions, finely chopped
  • 1 tsp sesame seeds

Preparation

  1. Heat 4 quart sauce pan over medium heat. Add oil, tofu, mushrooms and carrot and cook for 2 to 3 minutes.
  2. Add broth and begin to simmer.
  3. Decrease heat to medium-low and add miso. Whisk until miso is dissolved.
  4. Add sesame oil, tamari, and ginger and cook for 7 to 10 minutes.
  5. Serve garnished with scallions, a dash of sesame oil, and sesame seeds.
